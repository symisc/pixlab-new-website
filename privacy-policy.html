Collection of Personal Information

  • Personal information refers to any data that can identify you, such as your full name, phone number, or email address. You can explore our website freely without needing to provide personal information.
  • We utilize web analytics software to analyze site traffic, helping us understand the needs of our customers and visitors and continuously improve our site. However, the analytics software only collects anonymous, aggregated statistics.
  • If you choose to subscribe to our periodic email communications, we only require your email address as optional information. Some activities on our site, such as accessing a white paper, downloading trial software, or requesting further information about our Services or event registration, may require registration. During registration, we may ask for Required Information, including your name, company name, address, phone number, and email address. This data is used to fulfill your requests, inform you about products or services if requested, contact you for further details, verify our records, and send customer satisfaction surveys.
  • For our downloadable software products, certain Required Information may be needed for installation and execution. This data allows us to provide updates, conduct audits, and ensure license compliance. This browsing information includes standard data from your browser, such as browser vendor, device type (smartphone or computer), locale language, and your interactions with PixLab's web pages and clicked links.
